Dearest,

Everyone has a funny story to tell about someone whose name starts with J

I laughed at that thought before, not until I experienced it firsthand. Long story short, we met, Cupid's arrow missed.

I found myself falling deeply in love with you to the point that I will do anything to appease you. I tried to deny it but it still boiled down to one conclusionâ€“ you were my first love.

Back when I was a child, I thought that first love is something I watched in the movies; sweet, everlasting, and serene. And you happened.

You made me realize that sometimes, in life, we can not have it all. We can not force someone to see us the way we view them with rainbows and unicorns. We can not force them to love us back the way we give our hearts to them.

I just wanted you to know that I truly cherish you although you didn't.
