ablaze affections

cornelius

two hearts burn
as our skin ignites–
my chest roars in
a rhapsodic rhythm,
until fumes form from our lips,
as they meet: explode together.
and when your tender touch
set ember my doubts–
pulverizing them, turning into ashes.
i catch fire– like a matchstick;
bursting in love;
flaring.
