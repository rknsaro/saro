Starry Nights

Amidst the wandering, wild, and wondering crowds –
I was enthralled, mesmerized, and enchanted.
For I can not take away my eyes
From that majestic art –
Art that shines, that speaks right into my soul
Making me vulnerable,
Unable to withstand the fleeting feelings of being captivated.
And at that very moment, when I stare into it as gently as I could
I reminded myself,
Not to close the gap between us,
All I can do is to look at it,
Be bewitched by its beauty, its mystery, it's lovely scenery
Then again, I reminded myself
Not to touch nor lay a finger
On its delicate edges.
All I can do is admire it from afar;
Wander as I wonder how those bold brush strokes,
And vibrating blue, yellow and light green hues – in form of starry night skies,
Make my heart flutter, make me question myself and beg for an answer
On when admiration and contentment overlap with each other.



Silay

Pinilit na itunuwid ang
pumipilipit kong dila upang maging
Plantsado at praktisado ang
pagbitaw ng bawat parirala–
Tantsado ang mga hakbang,
Tiyak ang patutunguhan;
Tumpak, sipat na sipat sa
tapat ng mapupungay mong mata, ang bawat
Titig kong sumasabay sa ritmo ng
Himig ng pumipintig-pintig kong
pusong uhaw–
Gutom, naghihikahos sa pag-ibig mo.
Sa pagtangkang paglapit sa'yo'y
Pagbuhol ng dila't pagnginig ng paa ko–
Habang nakasilay sa
Unti-unting pagtayo, pagtalikod mo;
Pagtakbo papalayo.

Sana'y tumingin ka pabalik…



walang titulo

Paanong ba gang susukatin
ang kagandahang tinataglay;
Kung ang bilang at tugma ng
mga salita ay sinukat na at tinimbang, ngunit kulang–
Upang tumbasan ang tingkad ng
pamumukadkad ng
ngiti sa labi.
Marahil nga'y 'di sapat ang
mga sugnay ma't
pangungusap
para mapunan ang
patlang ng wika sa
pagbibigay linaw at kahulugan sa
iyong kariktan.



not a fever dream

I pinched myself-
to drag me back
from your swindling mirage.
And when the excruciating distress
sears, and dug deeper
on my skin to the bones and flesh
of my arms,
I know it was true.
It was you.
Who beamed a smile and
tell a tale-

You are not a fever dream,
but a glimpse of reality
on a deceitful day in April



sheets

Our bodies ignite,
as her touch set ablaze
my hunger
Yet every time I fixed my gaze
upon her face,
your image flash before my mind,
every time her lips devoured mine,
I crave your kisses.

And when midnight no longer reigns,
sheets
keep the bareness of my skin
but failed to cover
my burning desire
to yearn for your affection,
As she lay down beside me,
She caresses my scarred soul-
yet I closed my eyes
and drown in dreams
as I long for your embrace.



kay magdalena

hubarin mo ang iyong mga saplot,
nang marahan, mabagal-
indahin ang kirot at piliing
manoot at magpeklat sa balat
ang nagdurugo mong mga sugat,
hanggang mamukadkad
ang iyong katawan
sa kapirasong liwanag
at aking maaninag-

'wag takpan ang kahubaran-
at bigyang kalayaan ako
na baybayin ang bawat piraso,
ang mga anggulo,
ng mga lihim mong sikreto
sa mga pasa,
sa bakas ng natuyong luha,
sa mga kalmot,
at mga gunita
na pilit mong binabaon sa limot,
hayaan mong maging lunas
ang aking mga halik
at haplos
sa sakit na dinaranas,
ng puso mong naaagnas.



saro

perhaps, i could lie a thousand times
and tell myself
i forgot the tiniest details about you;
the way you fondle your hair
or the manner you brows brushed up
when i did something you found unamusing

i could lie a thousand times more
and tell myself,
i wouldn't see you as an art nor write poems about you no longer

i could lie again and again
yet, you will see it,
in my eyes,
behind those half grin, half smiles
you will see it,
the chaotic abomination of my desire
to dream of embracing you completely

perhaps, lying becomes something I grew accustomed to
then i broke boundaries between a lie and a truth of silently loving you
